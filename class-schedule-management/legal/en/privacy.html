Class Schedule Management App Privacy Policy
This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use our app:

  • Name and email address (via Google/Apple Sign-In if you provide)
  • Profile photos/avatars (via Google/Apple Sign-In if you provide)
  • Class schedule data
  • Student/member information you input
  • Course and session data
  • Learning progress and results
  • Settings and preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

1.3 Advertising Data

When using the free version with ads and if you consent:

  • Advertising ID for personalized ads
  • App usage patterns for ad targeting
  • Click and interaction data with advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Providing class schedule management services
  • Syncing data across your devices (Premium)
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Sending important app notifications
  • Customer support
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Processing subscription payments

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Google/Apple for authentication
  • Cloud storage and analytics services
  • Subscription management services
  • Google AdMob for advertising

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored locally on your device and optionally in cloud services
  • All data transmission uses encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Local data is protected by device security features
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Biometric authentication is supported for app access

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of advertising personalization

5.1 Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  • Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Or contact us via email below

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users 13+ years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and countries where our service providers operate.
